So You Think You Can Code?

When you click the Begin button you will be asked a series of questions.
A timer will start when a question appears. You then answer.
Once you have answered you will be presented with another question
If you answer incorrectly time will be deducted from the clock.
The quiz will end when all answers are completed or the timer reached to 0.
When the quiz is complete you will be able to type in your initials and see your score on the score board.
